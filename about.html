About Pretty Weird Art

We're Awesome. It's True.

You've seen our artworks. You know we draw Ninja Turtles fighting GI Joes. You know that Batman, Jason, and other cinematic and comic book legends have graced the walls of our digital galleries. What could have possessed you to even want to know anything more about us?

We're AWESOME, Ok?!! That's it. That's all you need to know about us. Trust us. We can go on about all the booooring details about how we grew up here and got "Eh-joo-kate-ed" there. Pretty Weird Art is the public face of illustrators Ethan and Rachel Mongin. Ethan and Rachel are a couple of self-diagnosed, obsessive-compulsive art makers who love comics, children's literature, and illustration. They are a tag-team dynamic duo who write and illustrate comics, conduct elementary school comic book clubs, and who work as freelance illustrators in Columbia, SC.
